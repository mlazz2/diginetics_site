What We'll Deliver

Threat Pattern Recognition

The platform discovers which sequences of network events are genuine precursors to breaches vs. benign noise. Just as it found causal chains in baseball (pitch environment → swing decisions → outcomes), it discovers attack chains in network telemetry.

Breach Likelihood Modeling

Continuous risk scoring based on discovered leading indicators, not static rule sets. The platform identifies which combinations of signals actually predict incidents, reducing false positives while catching real threats earlier.

Vulnerability Prioritization

Not all vulnerabilities are equal. The platform identifies which unpatched systems and misconfigurations are genuine leading indicators of breach risk vs. theoretical exposures that never get exploited in your specific environment.

Business Applications

Security Operations Centers

Cut through alert fatigue with prioritized, explainable threat signals. The platform tells analysts which alerts to investigate first and why, backed by discovered patterns across your environment's specific telemetry.

Enterprise Risk Management

Quantify cyber risk in business terms. Map which security investments actually reduce breach probability with specific magnitudes, so CISOs can justify budgets with data instead of fear.

Managed Security Providers

Per-client threat models that adapt to each environment. The same platform discovers unique risk patterns for each customer without custom engineering, scaling your analyst capacity.

Insurance & Underwriting

Data-driven cyber risk assessment for policy pricing. The platform's discovered risk factors provide quantified, auditable inputs for underwriting decisions instead of checkbox questionnaires.
