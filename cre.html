Property Valuation & Market Analysis

The same engine applied to commercial real estate — discovering what actually drives property values, cap rates, and NOI beyond what traditional comps-based analysis reveals.

Results Coming Soon

We're building the CRE configuration and sourcing property-level data. Results will be published here with full methodology, equations, and accuracy metrics once testing is complete.

What We Plan to Analyze

The CRE configuration will follow the same 3-layer causal hierarchy the system uses across all domains — leading indicators that drive intermediate metrics that determine outcomes.

Layer 1: Market & Location Drivers

Interest rates, local employment, population growth, new supply pipeline, submarket vacancy trends. The upstream forces that set the playing field for any individual property.

Layer 2: Property Performance

Occupancy rate, effective rent, tenant retention, operating expenses, capital expenditures, lease expiration schedule. The intermediate metrics that translate market conditions into property-level outcomes.

Layer 3: Valuation Outcomes

Net operating income (NOI), cap rate, property value, cash-on-cash return, debt service coverage ratio. The bottom-line metrics that determine investment performance.

What We Expect to Discover

Causal drivers of cap rate movement

Which specific market conditions cause cap rates to compress or expand, with what time delay, and through which intermediate property metrics? Readable equations showing the full causal chain.

Leading indicators for NOI shifts

Which metrics change first before NOI moves? The system will discover time-lagged feedback loops — early warning signals that a property's income is about to shift, days or weeks before it shows up in the financials.

Per-property causal maps

Just as the system found different optimization strategies for different baseball players, it will find different value drivers for different property types. An office building in Manhattan and a warehouse in Tampa will get unique causal structures.
