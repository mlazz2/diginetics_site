Why This Is Feasible

What we've already proven that could apply directly to CRE.

Commercial real estate is a time-series prediction problem with causal relationships between metrics. That's exactly the problem structure our platform has already solved three times.

Leading Indicator Discovery: Already Proven

In retail, the platform discovered that disposable vape average sale predicts store-wide revenue 3 days in advance, and glass average sale predicts it 10 days out. CRE has analogous leading indicators: vacancy rate changes, lease renewal velocity, and tenant credit shifts all precede NOI movements. This proven capability could apply to finding these timing relationships automatically.

Hidden Dependency Mapping: Already Proven

In retail, cigarette sales turned out to depend on vape traffic, not cigarette demand. In CRE, office valuations may depend on remote-work adoption rates in the tenant base, not office demand metrics. This suggests the platform could discover non-obvious cross-variable dependencies in CRE, where traditional comps miss structural drivers.

Per-Entity Adaptation: Already Proven

The platform produced unique performance drivers for each of 60 MLB players without being told what type of player it was analyzing. A Class A office in Manhattan and a suburban warehouse in Tampa are fundamentally different assets. This proven per-entity adaptation suggests each property could get its own causal model, not a generic sector average.

Regime Transition Detection: Already Proven

The platform detected 73,076 regime transitions across 60 baseball players, identifying tipping points between hot streaks and slumps. CRE markets undergo analogous regime shifts: from buyer's to seller's markets, from cap rate compression to expansion, from occupancy growth to contraction. This suggests the platform could detect these transitions with similar precision.

Compound Interactions: Already Proven

24% of discovered relationships in baseball were multi-factor interactions invisible to single-variable analysis. CRE valuations are notoriously multi-factor: a property's value depends on the interaction between interest rates, local employment, tenant mix, and lease terms simultaneously. Single-variable regression misses these. This proven capability could apply directly to CRE.

Zero Code Changes: Already Proven

The same platform went from baseball to retail to healthcare with only a configuration change. The data structure for CRE is different (property-level time series instead of player-level), but the underlying prediction problem is the same: discover causal relationships, find leading indicators, project forward, explain why. This suggests CRE could be the next natural application.

Where This Could Apply

Multifamily Rent Prediction

Local employment data, migration patterns, and permit activity could serve as leading indicators for effective rent changes at the property level. The same causal-chain discovery that mapped pitch environment to batting outcomes could map macro indicators to rent outcomes.

Office Occupancy Regime Shifts

Post-pandemic office markets are experiencing structural regime changes. The platform's regime-detection capability, proven across 73,076 transitions in baseball, could detect when a submarket is transitioning between occupancy regimes and help predict how far the shift will go.

Industrial/Logistics Demand Cycles

E-commerce penetration rates, port throughput, and inventory-to-sales ratios may serve as upstream drivers for industrial property demand. Based on proven results—where retail revenue shifts followed specific category signals by 3-10 days—these relationships could hold with quantifiable time delays in CRE as well.

Cap Rate Sensitivity Analysis

Cap rates are influenced by interest rates, risk premiums, and growth expectations simultaneously. The platform's compound-interaction discovery could identify which combinations of macro factors drive cap rate movements for specific property types, rather than relying on generic interest-rate correlations.

Tenant Credit Risk Cascades

A single tenant default can cascade through a property's financials. Tenant-level financial signals could serve as leading indicators for property-level NOI shifts, analogous to how individual metric shifts in baseball cascade through the performance chain.

Climate & ESG Risk Pricing

Flood risk, energy efficiency scores, and ESG compliance are increasingly priced into CRE valuations. The platform could quantify exactly how much these factors move value for specific property types in specific markets, replacing qualitative ESG narratives with quantified impact.
