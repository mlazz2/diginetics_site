Key Primary Drivers

The key structural relationships driving each market indicator. Every discovery is human-readable and domain-interpretable.

Primary Target - 98.90% Accuracy

price_yoy_change

Home price appreciation is fundamentally driven by the interaction of inflation pressure and market competitiveness. When CPI rises and homes sell near or above asking, prices accelerate. This held across COVID, the 2021-22 boom, rate hikes, and the 2024-25 normalization.

Strongest Discovery - 99.90% Accuracy

months_supply

One of the cleanest structural relationships discovered: housing inventory is almost entirely a function of mortgage rates. As rates rise, supply increases predictably. As rates fall, supply tightens. This relationship held stable across a decade of wildly different rate environments.

Supply-Demand Link - 99.85% Accuracy

new_home_sales

New home sales are a direct function of housing inventory. As supply rises, sales decline at a consistent, quantifiable rate. At 99.85% accuracy across a decade, this is one of the most reliable structural relationships in real estate. Tight supply drives high sales volume; excess supply suppresses it predictably.

Inflation Channel - 94.59% Accuracy

rent_yoy_change

Rents track inflation with a measurable amplification factor, consistently growing faster than CPI. This is the most direct inflation transmission channel in real estate. The lower accuracy (94.59%) reflects local rent dynamics that diverge from national CPI trends.

Rate Transmission - 99.86% Accuracy

mortgage_rate_30yr_roll7

The 30-year mortgage rate is structurally linked to the Fed funds rate with a remarkably stable credit spread. This held across zero-rate policy (2020), rapid tightening (2022–23), and plateau (2024–25). At 99.86% accuracy, the spread is remarkably stable - mortgage pricing is almost purely a function of Fed policy, not market sentiment.

Construction Pipeline - 99.89% Accuracy

housing_permits

Housing permits track new home sales with a consistent structural ratio. This reveals the structural pipeline: the construction industry issues roughly 2 permits for every completed sale (accounting for multi-unit projects, cancellations, and regulatory lead time). A 10% drop in new sales predicts a ~10% permit decline. 99.89% accuracy across a full housing cycle.

What You Can Do With This

This isn't a dashboard - it's a decision system. Here are the real problems real estate professionals can solve today with DIGINETICS CRE intelligence:

Investment Firms & REITs

Portfolio Allocation & Market Timing

DIGINETICS tells you exactly which macro variables drive price growth in each metro. When CPI trends up and sale-to-list tightens, the system quantifies the price impact. Use this to:

  • Rank metros by fundamental strength - San Francisco projects +5.50% while Miami projects +4.69%. The gap isn't a guess; it's the mathematical output of their specific CPI and competitiveness conditions.
  • Stress-test rate scenarios - The months supply relationship (99.90% accuracy) tells you: if rates drop from 6% to 5%, supply drops by ~1 month nationally. Feed that into your acquisition model.
  • Identify timing windows - The permits relationship (99.89% accuracy) links construction permits to sales volume. A permit slowdown predicts future supply constraints. Combined with rate projections, it flags metro-specific buying opportunities before they show up in comps.
Lenders & Underwriters

Dynamic Risk Assessment

Move beyond static underwriting models. The platform gives you forward-looking, metro-specific risk signals:

  • Quantified collateral risk - The new home sales discovery (99.85% accuracy) tells you exactly how sales respond to inventory shifts. The platform quantifies the precise impact of supply changes on sales volume. Chain that to the months supply discovery and you can forecast supply → sales → prices from a single rate move.
  • Early warning for distress - The rent growth relationship shows rent tracks CPI with a consistent amplification factor. When CPI decelerates, you know rental income is about to follow - weeks before it shows up in borrower financials.
  • Portfolio-wide monitoring - Run 31 metros simultaneously. Flag the ones where fundamentals are deteriorating before defaults materialize.
Property Management

Operational Decision Support

Know what's actually driving your market before you set next quarter's strategy:

  • Rent pricing intelligence - Rent growth tracks CPI with a consistent amplifier. The platform quantifies exactly what your rent increases should target to stay competitive based on current inflation. Push harder and you lose occupancy; too conservative and you leave money on the table.
  • Rate impact forecasting - The mortgage-to-Fed-funds discovery (99.86% accuracy) gives you a direct link from Fed policy to mortgage costs. Combine with months supply to project inventory changes from rate decisions before they hit the market.
  • Market-specific intelligence - Each metro has its own dynamics. Austin's price trajectory differs from Boston's for quantifiable reasons. Use metro-specific projections to tailor strategies for each property in your portfolio.
Brokers & Advisors

Data-Backed Market Intelligence

Differentiate your advisory practice with insights no one else has:

  • Quantified pitch decks - "Prices in this metro are projected to grow 5.1% based on current CPI and market competitiveness conditions" beats "we think the market is strong."
  • Causal explanations for clients - When a client asks "why is this market softening?", show them the discovered relationship: mortgage rates drive supply levels directly. Rates went up, supply followed. It's data, not opinion.
  • Cross-metro comparison tools - Rank 31 metros on any combination of 84 indicators. Show investors exactly where fundamentals are strongest.
Homebuyers & Sellers

Personal Real Estate Decisions

The same institutional-grade intelligence, accessible to individuals:

  • Is now a good time to buy? - The months supply relationship links inventory directly to mortgage rates. When rates drop, supply tightens and prices follow. DIGINETICS quantifies exactly when conditions shift from buyer-favorable to seller-favorable in your metro.
  • Where should I buy? - Compare 31 metros on projected price growth, rent-to-price ratios, and inventory trends. Data-driven relocation decisions.
  • Should I sell or hold? - The price momentum relationship tells you whether growth is accelerating or decelerating in your market. Combined with rate projections, it gives you a quantified hold vs. sell signal.
