What We'll Deliver

Demand Forecasting

Predict demand shifts before they propagate through the supply chain. The same platform that found 3-day early warning signals in retail discovers analogous leading indicators in procurement and logistics data.

Disruption Early Warning

Which upstream metrics change first before a disruption cascades downstream? Specific leading indicators with quantified time delays, giving operations teams advance warning to reroute or pre-stock.

Supplier Dependency Mapping

Just as we discovered hidden cross-department dependencies in retail (cigarette sales driven by vape traffic), the platform maps hidden dependencies between suppliers, routes, and facilities that traditional risk models miss.

Business Applications

Manufacturing & CPG

Map material dependencies across your supplier network. Know which component shortages will cascade into production delays, and how far in advance you can see them coming. Optimize safety stock by risk signal, not blanket rules.

Logistics & 3PL

Predict capacity crunches and routing bottlenecks before they impact delivery windows. Discover which operational metrics are true leading indicators for on-time performance vs. lagging noise.

Procurement

Quantify supplier risk with specific, data-backed signals instead of generic scorecards. Identify which vendors have hidden co-dependencies and which disruptions will hit multiple parts of your supply base simultaneously.

Retail & E-Commerce

Connect demand signals to inventory planning with discovered time delays. Know which product categories will surge and when, based on upstream signals the platform identifies automatically.
