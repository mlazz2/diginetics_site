What We'll Deliver

Claims Frequency Modeling

Predict which policyholders are most likely to file claims and when. The platform discovers which behavioral patterns and external factors are genuine leading indicators of claims activity vs. noise.

Loss Ratio Drivers

Which factors actually drive loss ratios for specific product lines? Not generic actuarial tables. Specific, quantified relationships between policyholder attributes, market conditions, and claims outcomes.

Risk Segmentation

Discover natural risk segments the platform finds in your data, not pre-defined categories. Just as it found that different MLB players need completely different strategies, it finds that different policyholder segments have completely different risk profiles.

Business Applications

P&C Carriers

Per-policy risk scoring with explainable drivers. Know exactly why a policy is rated high or low risk, with specific factors and magnitudes. Differentiate pricing based on discovered signals competitors can't see.

Life & Health Insurance

Discover which clinical and behavioral factors actually predict mortality and morbidity risk. The same platform that found high glucose prevalence as a readmission driver in healthcare applies directly to life underwriting.

Reinsurance

Portfolio-level risk assessment with per-segment driver analysis. Identify which cedant portfolios have hidden correlated risks and quantify aggregate exposure to regime shifts before they hit loss ratios.

Regulatory & Compliance

Every prediction and pricing decision comes with transparent, auditable reasoning. Built for Solvency II, IFRS 17, and state regulatory requirements. Replace "the model says so" with "here's exactly what drives this price."
