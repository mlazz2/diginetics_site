DIGINETICS Accepted into the Peachscore Data-Driven Accelerator

We have been accepted as a Fellow in the Peachscore Data-Driven Accelerator — the world's largest virtual, equity-free startup accelerator. Here's what that means for DIGINETICS and the companies we work with.

What Is the Peachscore Accelerator?

Peachscore operates the world's largest virtual and equity-free startup accelerator. Founded with backing from organizations including US Federal Government-funded national labs (LANL/LEEP Program), Plug & Play, Dealum, HubSpot for Startups, and Loyola Marymount University, the program accepts startups from any stage and any country into a structured 12-month growth program.

Unlike traditional accelerators that take equity, demand relocation, or run short-term sprints, Peachscore is fully virtual, takes zero equity, and provides daily mentorship and structured support over a full year. It is not a bootcamp — it is a sustained growth program designed to take companies from early traction to investor-ready.

What This Means for DIGINETICS

Investor Access

Direct access to Peachscore's network of 30,000+ investors and 300+ corporate executives. The program culminates in a Demo Day with up to $500K in funding opportunities, putting DIGINETICS in front of the capital needed to scale.

Daily Mentorship

Two hours of daily group office hours with experienced operators and domain experts. This is not periodic check-ins — it is daily, structured guidance on go-to-market strategy, product positioning, and fundraising execution.

AI-Driven Growth Insights

Peachscore uses its own data-driven platform to score and benchmark startups, providing actionable intelligence on where we stand relative to comparable companies and what metrics matter most for our next funding round.

Global Network

Access to a cohort of 60 startups from 16+ countries, plus the broader alumni network of 1,400+ companies. Cross-pollination of ideas, potential partnerships, and shared go-to-market learnings across industries and geographies.

Government & Enterprise Channels

Peachscore's anchor partners include US Federal Government labs and multiple government agencies. For a prediction platform with healthcare, real estate, and financial applications, these are direct channels to high-value enterprise deployments.

Equity-Free

Peachscore takes zero equity. DIGINETICS retains full ownership while accessing the same caliber of resources that equity-taking accelerators like Y Combinator and Techstars provide. Every dollar of future value stays with the founders and investors.

Why DIGINETICS Was Accepted

Published, Verified Results Across 4 Industries

Most early-stage startups apply to accelerators with a pitch deck and a vision. DIGINETICS applied with published, out-of-sample results: 96.2% accuracy across 60 MLB players, 0.18% error on hospital readmission prediction from 101K real patient encounters, 93.5% OOS accuracy on commercial real estate across 31 US metros, and cross-department causal discovery in retail — all generated by the same platform with zero code changes between domains. The breadth and depth of validated results demonstrated that the technology works across fundamentally different data types and industries.

One Platform, Any Domain

Peachscore's program is designed for companies with scalable, repeatable technology. DIGINETICS's core differentiator — a single prediction engine that works across any industry via configuration change alone — maps directly to the kind of compounding growth the accelerator is built to amplify. The platform doesn't need to be rebuilt for each new client or vertical.
