How It Works

Data in. Causal understanding out.

UPS runs a multi-stage pipeline that progressively refines its understanding of your data.

Ingest & Feature Engineering

Raw time-series data is ingested, validated, and transformed into rolling windows with temporal features.

Jump-Diffusion Modeling

Temporal dynamics are modeled with drift, volatility, and regime-shift detection to capture real-world behavior.

Symbolic Regression

Genetic programming discovers interpretable equations that explain relationships in the data — not black-box weights.

Causal Propagation

Discovered equations are composed into a causal hierarchy, propagating uncertainty through layers.

Probabilistic Forecasts

Monte Carlo simulation generates forecasts with confidence intervals, validated against held-out data at every stage.
