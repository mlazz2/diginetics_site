Key Discoveries

The platform finds what no one was looking for.

The most valuable output isn't accuracy numbers. It's the specific, quantified insights the platform discovers on its own.

Baseball / Comprehensive Report

System independently discovered the structure of hitting performance

Across 60 players, the platform independently identified the same performance structure that sabermetrics has spent decades building — without being given any baseball theory. It found it in hours.

Baseball / Comprehensive Report

Explains why hot streaks compound and slumps spiral

The system identified the specific performance patterns behind streaks and slumps: when power numbers rise, pitchers adjust, which creates more hittable pitches, which drives even higher power. The slump version works in reverse. Over 73,000 performance shift points were mapped across the roster.

Baseball / Comprehensive Report

Bobby Witt Jr.'s walk rate depends on two factors, not one

The platform found that Witt's walk rate is jointly driven by pitch velocity trends and swing decision patterns. Neither alone is sufficient. This kind of multi-factor insight is invisible to traditional stat analysis.

Baseball / Player Analysis

Luis Arraez: strikeout rate projected to four decimal places with zero error

The system produced a perfect K/PA projection for Arraez, identifying his extreme contact-first approach as the most stable, predictable signal in baseball. Cody Bellinger was projected at 99.0% accuracy. The system adapts to individuals without being told what type of player it's analyzing.

Retail

A single metric predicts store-wide revenue shifts 3 days in advance

When disposable vape average sale rises, the entire store's average sale follows 3 days later. Glass average sale is an even stronger signal at a 10-day lag, 2.2x more powerful. These are specific leading indicators with quantified time delays that no BI dashboard surfaces.

Retail

Cigarette sales don't depend on cigarette demand

Cigarette transactions are driven by the interaction between disposable vape volume and cigarillo traffic. If vape supply drops, cigarette baskets decline even if cigarette inventory is fully stocked. The platform discovers hidden cross-department dependencies that rewrite how you plan inventory and supply chains.

Healthcare

0.02 percentage point error on the problem the entire industry has spent a decade trying to solve

Published readmission models achieve AUC of 0.60-0.76 and rate-level errors of 2-5 percentage points. A 2025 medRxiv study found no approach (ML, LLMs, or physician panels) achieved clinical-grade reliability. The platform predicted the 30-day readmission rate at 10.78% vs. 10.80% actual. 20x more accurate than the historical mean. 60x more accurate than naive persistence.

Healthcare

Independently discovered the primary readmission driver

Given 126 clinical metrics and zero guidance, the platform identified high glucose prevalence adjusted by demographic factors as the dominant driver of 30-day readmission. This aligns with published clinical literature. The system found what took clinical researchers years to establish.

Cross-Domain

Patterns discovered in baseball applied directly to healthcare and retail

Insights from the baseball analysis translated directly into better results in other domains. The same types of performance patterns found in hitting — cascading effects, hidden dependencies, combined factors — showed up in retail cross-sell data and healthcare readmission patterns.
