Key Insights

What we discovered across every industry.

The most valuable output isn't just accuracy numbers. It's the specific, actionable insights the platform uncovers that no one was looking for.

Baseball

Plate discipline, not swing mechanics, drives Aaron Judge's power

For every 1% reduction in chase rate, Judge's exit velocity increases by 9.5 mph. The platform identified discipline as the dominant lever: a coaching directive backed by 10 years of data.

Baseball

Different players need completely different strategies

Judge's performance is driven by plate discipline. Mookie Betts' is driven by launch angle management. The platform discovered this automatically, proving it adapts to individuals without being told what to look for.

Retail

Products secretly drive each other's sales, and the platform finds which ones

Vape device transactions are driven by cigarillo traffic, not vape demand. Cigarette sales depend on a vape-cigarillo interaction. These hidden cross-sell dependencies change how you merchandise, promote, and plan inventory across departments.

Retail

Anticipate store-wide sales spikes 3 days before they hit

When disposable vape average sale rises, the entire store's average sale follows 3 days later. One metric, daily tracking, advance warning of store-wide revenue shifts.

Healthcare

Predicted hospital readmission rate within 0.02 percentage points

On 101,766 real patient encounters from 130 US hospitals, the platform achieved 20x better accuracy than historical averages. The entire healthcare industry has been working on this problem for a decade.

High glucose prevalence identified as primary readmission driver

The platform independently discovered that high glucose prevalence and demographic factors drive 30-day readmission rates, aligning with published clinical literature, without being given any clinical hypotheses.
