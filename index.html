What We Do

Your data has answers buried in it. We dig them out.

Most analytics tools tell you what happened. We tell you what's going to happen, what's causing it, and what you can do about it.

Find the real drivers

Your business has metrics that drive other metrics — but which ones? And by how much? Our engine discovers causal relationships automatically, with specific magnitudes and time delays. Not "these correlate" but "when X changes by 1, Y changes by 3.2 after 10 days."

Get answers you can read

Every insight comes as a human-readable equation you can verify yourself. Not a neural network with a billion hidden weights — an actual formula. Your team can read it, challenge it, and trust it. If the math doesn't make sense, you'll know.

See what's coming

Probabilistic forecasts with honest confidence bounds. We tell you what we're confident about and where the uncertainty is. No false precision — if the data doesn't support a tight prediction, we say so.

Discover what you didn't know to look for

The most valuable findings aren't the ones you expected. Our engine discovers cross-category dependencies, hidden feedback loops, and time-lagged effects that no dashboard or BI tool will ever show you. Insights you didn't know existed in your own data.

One engine, your industry

We don't rebuild the system for each client. The same engine works across sports, retail, healthcare, real estate, and more. You bring your data and your domain knowledge — we bring the prediction engine. Up and running in days, not months.

Know what lever to pull

Don't just know that revenue will drop — know that it's because metric A shifted, which caused metric B to move 3 days later, which is now dragging metric C down. Our system traces the full causal chain so you know exactly where to intervene.
